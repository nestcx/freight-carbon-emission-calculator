Welcome to Cargo2
Cargo2 is a free and online educational tool created to provide Australian freight shippers and carriers with a simple easy-to-use tool that will estimate how much greenhouse gasses their shipments are producing.

We created Cargo2 with a scalable interface to be used by freight companies, moving substantial products across the country, wanting comprehensible information about each shipment’s harmful emissions and also giving individuals who are curious an educational opportunity to learn about how much environmental impact their online orders have.

Shipments with Cargo2
Entering, saving and viewing shipments is easily achieved with our intuitive user interface design, and with file uploading, Cargo2 can cater for larger companies sending and receiving thousands of shipments. Simply create or login with a free account and start today.

Comprehensive Shipments Reports
We understand that the data behind CO2 emissions can be difficult to read. That’s why Cargo2 has a user-friendly, clean dashboard design that makes visualising potential financial, and environmental harm easy. Our information is broken down to allow users to make informed decisions and understand their impact on our environment.

Comparing your Shipments
Comparing and having a reference point are important aids in fully understanding the environmental impact the shipments in our day to day lives have. With our compare shipments tool, we wanted users to be able to compare and see the differences each shipment has, giving them the tools and knowledge to understand the weight of their environmental impact and take another step closer to environmental healthier choices.
